Privacy Policy for Tech Prep

Last Updated: February 23, 2026

Tech Prep ("we," "our," or "us") operates the Tech Prep mobile application (the "App"). This Privacy Policy explains how information is collected, used, and protected when you use the App.

By using Tech Prep, you agree to the practices described in this Privacy Policy.

1. Information We Collect

A. Learning Progress and Performance Data

  • The App records your quiz answers, scores, streaks, and progress through question sets.
  • Performance analytics (accuracy rates, topic strengths/weaknesses) are calculated and stored locally on your device.
  • This data is used solely to personalise your study experience and track your readiness.

B. Preferences and Settings

  • Study preferences such as selected topics, difficulty level, and notification settings are stored locally.

C. Device and Diagnostic Data

  • The App may collect basic device information (OS version, device model) for crash reporting and stability improvements.
  • Diagnostic logs are generated locally and are not transmitted without your consent.

D. Data You Provide

  • The App does not require account creation or personal information to function.
  • If you contact us for support, we may collect your email address and message content solely to respond to your inquiry.

2. How We Use Information

  • Track your learning progress and display performance trends.
  • Recommend topics and questions based on your strengths and weaknesses.
  • Maintain streaks and milestones to keep you motivated.
  • Improve app stability and content quality.

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal data or learning progress with third parties for marketing purposes. Data may be disclosed only when required by law.

4. Third-Party Services

  • If the App displays ads, the ad provider may collect standard advertising identifiers as described in their own privacy policy.
  • The App may use analytics tools (e.g., Firebase Analytics) to collect anonymised usage data to improve content and features.

5. Data Storage and Retention

  • All progress, scores, and settings are stored locally on your device.
  • Data remains on your device until you choose to reset it or uninstall the App.
  • Uninstalling the App removes all locally stored data.

6. Your Choices

  • You can reset your progress from within the App settings.
  • You can opt out of personalised advertising through your device's ad settings.
  • You can uninstall the App at any time to remove all stored data.

7. Children's Privacy

The App is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

8. Security

We take reasonable measures to protect the data stored by the App. However, no method of electronic storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updated versions will be posted on this page with a revised "Last Updated" date.
