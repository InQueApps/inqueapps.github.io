Privacy Policy for Rate My Fit

Last Updated: February 23, 2026

Rate My Fit ("we," "our," or "us") operates the Rate My Fit mobile application (the "App"). This Privacy Policy explains how information is collected, used, and protected when you use the App.

By using Rate My Fit, you agree to the practices described in this Privacy Policy.

1. Information We Collect

A. Photos and Outfit Images

  • When you post an outfit for feedback, the image is uploaded to our servers so other users can view and rate it.
  • You choose which photos to share; the App does not access your photo library without permission.
  • You can delete your posted images at any time from within the App.

B. Account Information

  • If account creation is offered, we may collect a username, email address, or social sign-in token.
  • This information is used solely for authentication and to associate your posts and ratings.

C. User-Generated Content

  • Ratings, comments, and feedback you provide on other users' outfits are stored on our servers.
  • Your interactions may be visible to other users of the App.

D. Device and Diagnostic Data

  • The App may collect basic device information (OS version, device model) for crash reporting and stability improvements.
  • Diagnostic logs are generated locally and are not transmitted without your consent.

2. How We Use Information

  • Display your posted outfits to other users for ratings and feedback.
  • Provide personalised style suggestions based on community feedback trends.
  • Improve app stability, performance, and user experience.
  • Moderate content to maintain community standards.

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell or trade your personal data to third parties for marketing purposes. Posted outfit images and associated ratings are visible to other App users as part of the community experience. Data may be disclosed when required by law.

4. Third-Party Services

  • The App may use cloud storage and backend services to host user-uploaded images.
  • If the App displays ads, the ad provider may collect standard advertising identifiers as described in their own privacy policy.
  • The App may use analytics tools to collect anonymised usage data to improve the experience.

5. Data Storage and Retention

  • Uploaded images and account data are stored on secure servers.
  • You can delete your posts and account data from within the App.
  • Uninstalling the App removes locally cached data; server-stored content can be deleted by contacting us or through in-app settings.

6. Your Choices

  • You can deny or revoke camera and photo library permissions at any time through your device settings.
  • You can delete individual posts or your entire account from within the App.
  • You can opt out of personalised advertising through your device's ad settings.

7. Children's Privacy

The App is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

8. Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your data, including encryption for data in transit and at rest. However, no method of electronic transmission or storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updated versions will be posted on this page with a revised "Last Updated" date.
