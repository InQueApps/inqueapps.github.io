Privacy Policy for Pendular

Last Updated: February 23, 2026

Pendular ("we," "our," or "us") operates the Pendular mobile application (the "App"). This Privacy Policy explains how information is collected, used, and protected when you use the App.

By using Pendular, you agree to the practices described in this Privacy Policy.

1. Information We Collect

A. Device and Diagnostic Data

  • The App may collect basic device information (OS version, device model) for crash reporting and stability improvements.
  • Diagnostic logs are generated locally and are not transmitted without your consent.

B. Gameplay Data

  • The App may store gameplay progress, scores, and settings locally on your device.
  • No gameplay data is transmitted to external servers unless you opt in to a leaderboard or sharing feature.

C. Data You Provide

  • The App does not require account creation or personal information to function.
  • If you contact us for support, we may collect your email address and message content solely to respond to your inquiry.

2. How We Use Information

  • Save and restore your game progress and preferences.
  • Improve app stability and performance through anonymized crash reports.
  • Provide customer support when requested.

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal data with third parties for marketing purposes. Data may be disclosed only when required by law (for example, in response to a valid legal process).

4. Third-Party Services

  • The App may use third-party advertising networks. These services may collect standard advertising identifiers as described in their own privacy policies.
  • The App may use analytics tools (e.g., Firebase Analytics) to collect anonymized usage data to improve gameplay experience.

5. Data Storage and Retention

  • All game progress and settings are stored locally on your device.
  • Data remains on your device until you choose to delete it or uninstall the App.
  • Uninstalling the App removes all locally stored data.

6. Your Choices

  • You can reset your game progress from within the App settings.
  • You can opt out of personalized advertising through your device's ad settings.
  • You can uninstall the App at any time to remove all stored data.

7. Children's Privacy

The App is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

8. Security

We take reasonable measures to protect the data stored by the App. However, no method of electronic storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updated versions will be posted on this page with a revised "Last Updated" date.
