Privacy Policy for AnyConvert

Last Updated: February 23, 2026

AnyConvert ("we," "our," or "us") operates the AnyConvert mobile application (the "App"), formerly distributed as Universal Media Converter. This Privacy Policy explains how information is collected, used, and protected when you use the App.

By using AnyConvert, you agree to the practices described in this Privacy Policy.

1. Information We Collect

A. Files You Convert

  • The App accesses files you select for conversion (images, audio, video, documents) only when you initiate a conversion.
  • All file processing happens locally on your device. Files are not uploaded to external servers.
  • The App does not access files beyond those you explicitly choose.

B. Storage Access

  • The App requires storage permissions to read source files and write converted output files.
  • It does not scan, index, or catalogue your storage contents.

C. Device and Diagnostic Data

  • The App may collect basic device information (OS version, device model) for crash reporting and stability improvements.
  • Diagnostic logs are generated locally and are not transmitted without your consent.

D. Data You Provide

  • The App does not require account creation or personal information to function.
  • If you contact us for support, we may collect your email address and message content solely to respond to your inquiry.

2. How We Use Information

  • Convert files between supported formats as requested by you.
  • Save converted output to your device storage.
  • Improve app stability, conversion quality, and format support.

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal data or files with third parties for marketing purposes. Your files never leave your device during the conversion process. Data may be disclosed only when required by law.

4. Third-Party Services

  • The App may use open-source libraries (e.g., FFmpeg) for media processing. These libraries run locally and do not transmit data.
  • If the App displays ads, the ad provider may collect standard advertising identifiers as described in their own privacy policy.
  • The App may use analytics tools to collect anonymised usage data (e.g., which format conversions are most popular) to improve features.

5. Data Storage and Retention

  • Source files remain untouched; converted files are saved to a location you specify.
  • The App does not maintain a history of conversions on any external server.
  • Uninstalling the App removes the application and its cache; converted files you saved remain in your storage.

6. Your Choices

  • You can deny or revoke storage permissions at any time through your device settings; the App will not be able to read or write files without them.
  • You can delete converted files at any time using your device's file manager.
  • You can opt out of personalised advertising through your device's ad settings.

7. Children's Privacy

The App is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

8. Security

We take reasonable measures to protect the data processed by the App. Since all conversions happen locally on your device, your files are never exposed to network-based threats during processing.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updated versions will be posted on this page with a revised "Last Updated" date.
