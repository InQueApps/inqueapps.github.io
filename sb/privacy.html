Privacy Policy for Ski Bum

Last Updated: February 23, 2026

Ski Bum ("we," "our," or "us") operates the Ski Bum mobile application (the "App"). This Privacy Policy explains how information is collected, used, and protected when you use the App.

By using Ski Bum, you agree to the practices described in this Privacy Policy.

1. Information We Collect

A. Location / GPS Data

  • The App uses your device's GPS to record your skiing routes, including coordinates, altitude, speed, and timestamps.
  • Location data is collected only while you are actively recording a ski session.
  • Route data is stored locally on your device unless you choose to export or share it.

B. Device and Diagnostic Data

  • The App may collect basic device information (OS version, device model) for crash reporting and stability improvements.
  • Diagnostic logs are generated locally and are not transmitted without your consent.

C. Data You Provide

  • Any names, labels, or notes you add to saved routes are stored locally on your device.
  • The App does not require account creation or personal information to function.

2. How We Use Information

  • Record and display your skiing routes on a map.
  • Calculate session statistics such as distance, elevation, speed, and duration.
  • Allow you to review, export, and share your saved routes.
  • Improve app stability and performance.

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal or location data with third parties for marketing purposes. Data may be disclosed only when required by law (for example, in response to a valid legal process).

4. Third-Party Services

  • The App may use third-party map providers (e.g., Google Maps, Mapbox) to render route maps. These services may receive anonymized location data as part of map tile requests.
  • If the App displays ads, the ad provider may collect standard advertising identifiers as described in their own privacy policy.

5. Data Storage and Retention

  • All route and session data is stored locally on your device.
  • Data remains on your device until you choose to delete it.
  • Uninstalling the App removes all locally stored data.

6. Your Choices

  • You can deny or revoke location permissions at any time through your device settings; however, route recording will not function without GPS access.
  • You can delete any saved route from within the App.
  • You control whether to export or share your route data.

7. Children's Privacy

The App is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

8. Security

We take reasonable measures to protect the data stored by the App. However, no method of electronic storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updated versions will be posted on this page with a revised "Last Updated" date.
